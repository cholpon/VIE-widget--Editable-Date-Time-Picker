Avatar is a science fiction film directed by James Cameron, a Canadian film director and film producer. Cameron was born in Kapuskasing, Ontario on August 16, 1954 and is famous for his previous works, such as "The Terminator"(1984), "Aliens"(1986), "True Lies"(1994) and "Titanic"(1997). To launch a promotion campaign for Avatar, Cameron appeared at San Diego Comic-Con International, a four-day convention that was held from July 23th, 2009 to July 27th, 2009. On December 10, 2009 the film premiered in London. It received positive critical reviews worldwide and quickly became the highest-grossing film of all time. The film's production company was Lightstorm Entertainment.
